title:  "Grayscale Litecoin Trust (LTCN) sees 500,000 LTC inflows in 2024"
---
As reported on SEC filings, the Grayscale Litecoin Trust (LTCN) saw inflows of 500,000 in the year of 2024. The total assets under management rose from `1,486,811 LTC` on January 1st, 2024 to `2,028,245 LTC` by January 1st, 2025.
