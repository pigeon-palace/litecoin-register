title:  "Grayscale Litecoin Trust (LTCN) gains more than 500,000 LTC in 2024"
---
As reported on SEC filings, the Grayscale Litecoin Trust (LTCN) saw net inflows of more than 500,000 litecoin in the year of 2024. The total assets under management rose from `1,486,811 LTC` on January 1st, 2024 to `2,028,245 LTC` by January 1st, 2025 an increase of `541,434 LTC`. See more information here: [https://litecoinregister.com/profile/ltcn.html](https://litecoinregister.com/profile/ltcn.html)
